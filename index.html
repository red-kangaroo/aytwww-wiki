What does this do?

To the left, you can select a date and a list of the most popular pages on Wikipedia for that day will be displayed. Clink on an article that would interest you and a short excerpt will be shown right here. No need to waste your time reading the whole thing!

To the right, you can see the history of articles you tried out.
