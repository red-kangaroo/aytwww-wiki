What does this do?

To the left, you can select a date and a list of the most popular pages on Wikipedia for that day will be displayed. Click on an article that would interest you and a short excerpt will be shown right here. No need to waste your time reading the whole thing on Wikipedia and getting distracted by all the hyperlinks!

To the right, you can see the history of articles you tried out. Click on them to read them again.

About

The ideal tool for the lazy Wikipedia lover!

Wikipedia Companion lets you keep tabs on what all the fuss is about on Wikipedia right now, without you needing to go on Wikipedia and risking getting sucked down the labyrinth of endless interesting hyperlinks.

Select a date and you will be presented with a list of the most popular pages for that day by the number of visitors. You can click on an interesting title to display an excerpt from that page. A history of your articles will also be saved, so that you can come back to them later.
